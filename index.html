Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

Is the pilot really free?

Yes—no credit card required. We simply ask for candid feedback to help us improve the platform.

Which tests are covered today?

Digital SAT English & Math, with ACT following this summer. We're also planning to add AP exam content in the near future.

How many tutors and students can I add during the pilot?

During the pilot, you can add up to 5 tutors and 50 students to fully test the platform's capabilities.

Do you provide training for our tutors?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive onboarding sessions and ongoing support to ensure your team can maximize the platform's benefits.

What happens after the pilot period?

Pilot partners will receive priority access to our paid plans at special founding member pricing.

Is the platform compatible with our existing systems?

Our platform is designed as a standalone solution, but we offer data export options that work with most common tutoring management systems.

How secure is student data on your platform?

We take data security extremely seriously. All data is encrypted, and we're fully FERPA compliant. We never share or sell student information.
